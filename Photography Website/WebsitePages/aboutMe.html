ABOUT ME




Hi! I'm Francis!
I'm a photographer based in Dundalk, Co. Louth. I joined the Irish Defence Forces and after spending eight years in the Army, I found that my love of people and learning the stories of others led me to study Psychology for my undergrad degree. I stumbled upon a wedding photography studio looking for an assistant and thought it would be a great part-time job. Shortly after working there, I realized that photography could be my career. I dove headfirst into the wedding photography world. I’m so happy that my love of photography has turned into what it is today, and that I still get to learn about other people the way I did with Psychology – I’ve been lucky enough to photograph couples in love now for 7 years and couldn’t imagine doing anything else.

I have the best job in the world! I love getting to know people, learning their stories and being trusted to document their lives in photographs that will last a lifetime.

My dogs are the best dogs and they are my life. It is true when they say, "A dog is a man's best friend".

Skiing trips are a regular thing for me, my friends and my family.

I love horror films but equally love films/shows that will make me laugh the entire time (some of my favourite films are A Quite Place, The Conjuring and Friends)

My beverages of choice is Whiskey on the rocks or a nice fresh pint of Guiness (Let’s grab a drink together!)

Family is the most important thing to me in my life. There are a total of 34 Lucchesi's in the family. I go on regular trips to Italy to meet up with the entire famiglia(family)

Football is a big part of my life. I am a massive Liverpool fan (hopefully I wont loose business because of this :) I am also a Roma fan but not by choice (Grandpa enforces it :)
