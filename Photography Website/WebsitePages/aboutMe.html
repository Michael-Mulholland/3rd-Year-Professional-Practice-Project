Hi! I'm Francis!
I'm a wedding and engagement photographer based in Dundalk, Co. Louth.
I’ve always loved photography, but wedding photography wasn’t actually something I originally had on my radar as a career. I was always wanted to join the Irish Defence Forces and after spending eight years in the Army, I found that my love of people and learning the stories of others led me to study Psychology for my undergrad degree. While applying to colleges, I stumbled upon a wedding photography studio looking for an assistant and thought it would be a great part-time job. However, shortly after working there, I connected the dots and realized that photography could be my career. I quickly placed college on hold and dove headfirst into the wedding photography world. I’m so happy that my love of photography has turned into what it is today, and that I still get to learn about other people the way I did with Psychology – I’ve been lucky enough to photograph couples in love now for 7 years and couldn’t imagine doing anything else.
